ABOUT US LAGOS UX

INNOVATION IS IN OUR DNA

Lagos UX began as an internal design group at our parent company ICS and quickly evolved into a new breed of design agency. Our internal culture prizes exploration and amplifies creativity. We imagine what could be, and have the deep skills and experience to transform this vision to reality.

We go to great lengths to ensure user experience design is a keystone of innovative, smart products. If you’re looking for a design partner to help you create products that disrupt industries and push the boundaries of what’s possible, we should talk.

MEET OUR TEAM

Our talented UX and visual designers thrive in our fast-paced environment where each day brings a new challenge, whether designing the UI for an autonomous vehicle one day and a gesture-controlled medical device the next. Driven to learn and continuously enhance their skills, our team is redefining what full-stack UX means. So, whatever technological innovations the future brings, we’ll be right there designing groundbreaking products and user experiences that move the needle for our customers

JOIN US

Do you want to work with a team that pushes the boundaries of traditional UX? Deliver rich, layered experiences in high-stakes industries or projects? Enjoy a new challenge daily? Whether you’re a proven leader or a recent grad embarking on a creative career, Lagos UX is always looking for outstanding, talented individuals to help us move design and our business forward.
